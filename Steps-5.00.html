How to create denormalize data? 5

Denormalization is a strategy or a process used on a previously normalized database to increase performance in the OLAP environment. It is the process of   improving the read performance of a database, by adding redundant copies of data or by grouping data. One of the main purpose of denormalization is to reduced the number of physical tables that must be assessed to retrieve the desire data by reducing the number of joins needed to derive a query.
